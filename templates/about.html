03 : About Us

Background:

Vesta was the goddess of the hearth, the home, and domestic life in the Roman religion (idenitified with the Greek goddess Hestia). She was the first-born of the titans Kronos and Rhea.

How we started?

Founders, Elie and Ray, thought of a platform that enables doctors and patients interact and provide medical services in the comfort of one's home. Vesta through its various affiliates and channels, facilitates essential personnel and equipments for its services.

Vision and Mission:

Stay safe and healthy at home. Vesta aims to provide value and personalized healthcare services in your own place.
